Get Personalised Stock Suggestions

This applications performs the following functions:

  • Perform risk analysis of user portfolios, and then classifies them as conservative, moderate, aggressive
  • Find preferred categories of companies from portfolio
  • Perform risk analysis companies
  • Match users to companies from their categories and preferred categories
  • Preform a short term analysis of companies using Support Vector Regression
  • Use Relative Strength Index to provide medium term analysis of companies

To get started select either user list or company list from the menu.
